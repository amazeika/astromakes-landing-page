Roadmap

ASTROmakes is a work in progress that started on January 2024. I have since taking it further one step at time ...

  • Winter 2024

    The idea

    Completion of a couple of personal makes for improving my astrophotography setup. They turned out so great that this idea of them becoming actual products grew strong. ASTROmakes was born.

  • Spring 2024

    Legal aspects and initial investments

    Chat with my accountant about the project, the legal aspects and the required investments for the development of the brand. Initial investment consisted on a CNC machine for aluminum and carbon fiber machining, one big format 3D printer as well as some BambuLab machines capable of printing fast and flawlessly. Electronics measurement equipment was also added to the list completing the required instrumentation needed for getting the job done.

  • Summer 2024 - Autum 2024

    The workshop

    From hobbie to serious business, it's time to make room for all these machines an a proper work environment to get the job done. Let's build the workshop of my dreams!

  • Autum 2024 - Spring 2025

    Design revisions

    ASTROmakes aims to provide good value and quality products you will enjoy. This period is devoted on making these initial working concepts better and transform them into actual affordable products.

    Launch time!
