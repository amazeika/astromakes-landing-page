Roadmap

To put things a bit more in perspective on and when to expect the first batches of products out

  • Winter 2024

    The idea

    Completion of a couple of personal makes for improving my astrophotography setup. They turned out so great that I had to have this discussion with wife about the possibility of making them actual products. ASTROmakes was born.

  • Spring 2024

    Legal aspects and initial investments

    Chat with my accountant about the project, the legal aspects and the required investments for the development of the brand. Initial investment consisted on a CNC machine for aluminum and carbon fiber machining, one big format 3D printer as well as some BambuLab machines as to provide the best quality and results. ASTROmakes also disposes of a capable electronics lab for measurements and design
